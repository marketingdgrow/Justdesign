About JD

Chennai's Best Interior Designers for Homes, Offices and Modular Kitchens

Just Design Interiors is a full-service interior design company in Chennai, founded in 2009. We plan, design and build interiors for homes, offices and modular kitchens — entirely in-house, from the first site visit to the final handover. No subcontractors. No handoffs. One team responsible for everything.

Over 15 years, we have completed 250+ spaces across Chennai — from 1BHK flats in Velachery to corporate offices on OMR, from modular kitchens in Anna Nagar to full home interiors in Adyar. Every project is designed around the people who will live or work in it — not around a catalogue or a trend.

How the Top Interior Designers in Chennai Build Your Space

Every project at Just Design Interiors follows a fixed process, whether it is a single bedroom or a full office floor. This is how we keep every project on budget, on time and exactly as agreed.

Structured planning. In-house execution. Clean handover.

01

Site Visit and Brief

We visit your space in Chennai, walk through every room, and spend time understanding how you actually use the space before we draw or suggest anything.

02

Concept and Layout

Our design team prepares a custom layout with 3D visualisations and a full itemised cost sheet. You see the plan and the price before anything moves forward.

03

Material Selection

Every material, finish and fitting is chosen with you, from cabinet hardware to wall textures, with everything confirmed in writing before production begins.

04

In-House Production

Our workshop fabricates every unit, including furniture, cabinetry and modular frames, at our own facility. Nothing is outsourced and nothing leaves our hands until it is ready.

05

Site Fixing and Execution

The same team that built your pieces installs them at your home or office in Chennai. No third-party contractors and no gaps between what was built and what was planned.

06

Final Walkthrough and Handover

We walk through every room with you on handover day, checking every corner against the approved design. Your space is handed over clean, complete and on the date agreed at the start.
