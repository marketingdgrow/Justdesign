Kitchen layouts we design

L-Shaped Kitchen

Two adjacent walls create a natural work triangle. Opens to a nearby room - ideal for families who cook and talk at the same time.

U-Shaped Kitchen

Counter space on three sides - the most storage-efficient layout for medium and large kitchens with more than one cook.

G-Shaped Kitchen

A U-shape with a peninsula - extra counter and cabinet space for kitchens that need a dedicated prep zone.

Straight Kitchen

Single-wall layout for compact homes. Everything on one line - works well in studio flats and 1BHK kitchens in Chennai.

Island Kitchen

A central island adds a prep zone, breakfast counter or informal seating - for kitchens with enough floor space to breathe.

Parallel Kitchen

Two facing walls with a work corridor between them - efficient for one cook with a clear distinction between prep and cooking zones.
